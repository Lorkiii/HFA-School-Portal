About

Building Christ-centered futures since 2021

Our History

Founded in 2021 by the late Mr. Wilfredo G. Ventura, Holy Family Academy began its journey in Sta. Maria, Bulacan. Under the leadership of President Ms. Rizza I. Ventura, we've grown to two campuses:

Main Campus Sitio Hulo, Balasing, Sta. Maria, Bulacan
Catmon Campus Sitio Hulo, Catmon, Sta. Maria, Bulacan (offering JHS to SHS programs)

Our students - proudly called FAMILIANS - enjoy diverse academic tracks including STEM, ABM, GAS, HUMSS, and TVL-ICT.

Our Mission

Holy Family Academy of Sta. Maria Bulacan Inc, is committed to provide a high quality education that prepares students to excel academically, culturally, and athletically. We strive to develop effective communicators and productive citizens who integrate faith and love of family into their daily lives. Our holistic approach ensures that each student is well-rounded, capable, and ready to contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

Holy Family Academy of Sta. Maria Bulacan Inc. aspires to graduates who possess the ability to understand and perform effectively, think and analyze critically, and evaluate situations with clarity. Our graduates will be well-prepared to excel in all aspects of life, equipped with the knowledge, practical skills, and strong values necessary to thrive in any situation
