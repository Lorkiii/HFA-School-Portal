Enrollment Process

1

Prepare Required Documents

Gather all necessary documents listed below. Make sure all documents are valid and complete.

2

Choose Your Track

Select from Academic (STEM, ABM, HUMSS, GAS) or Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) tracks based on your interests and career goals.

3

Fill Out Enrollment Form

Complete the online enrollment form when the enrollment period is open. Provide accurate information and track preference.

4

Submit to School Office

Submit your completed form and documents to the school registrar's office during office hours.

5

Wait for Confirmation

The school will review your application and contact you for confirmation and next steps.
